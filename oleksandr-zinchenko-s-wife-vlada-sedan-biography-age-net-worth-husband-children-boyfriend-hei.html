    Her magnetic personality and professional demeanor have made her popular among football enthusiasts and fans.

    Early Life & Education

    Vlada Sedan was born on October 20, 1995, in Vinnytsia, Ukraine, and would be clocking 28 years old as of October 2023.

    She grew up alongside her parents and siblings in her hometown, although specific information about her family remains undisclosed.

    Vlada attended a private school for her early education and later pursued her high school education at School Gymnasium No. 2 in Vinnytsia.

    After completing high school, she continued her studies at the prestigious Taras Shevchenko National University.

    Personal Life

    Vlada Sedan’s romantic journey led her to Oleksandr Zinchenko, the talented Ukrainian footballer who plays for Manchester City and the Ukraine national team.

    Their relationship became the talk of the town after they were first seen kissing each other in an interview following Ukraine’s victory.

    The couple’s love blossomed, and they decided to tie the knot in August 2020. Their wedding ceremony occurred at the 70,000-capacity Olympic Stadium in Kiev, where Zinchenko proposed to Vlada in front of a heart-shaped display of red roses.

    In the subsequent year, Vlada and Oleksandr welcomed their first daughter in August 2021. And in May 2023, they joyfully announced that they were expecting their second child.

    Career

    Vlada Sedan’s TV presenter and journalist career gained momentum when she joined Channel Football 1/2 as a sports reporter.

    Her exceptional interviewing skills allowed her to interact with football legends like Cristiano Ronaldo and Andriy Shevchenko.

    As a result of her charming presence and professional demeanor, Vlada became a renowned sports media personality in Ukraine.

    Apart from her career in journalism, Vlada also dabbles in modeling, where her stunning figure and charisma have earned her recognition in the fashion industry.

    Additionally, she serves as an ambassador for the Puma apparel company, further solidifying her presence in the sports world.
