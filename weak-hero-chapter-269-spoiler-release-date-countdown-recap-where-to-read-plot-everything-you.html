    Plotlines and Story

    Weak Hero is a webtoon that combines action, drama, and comedy, with a focus on the themes of bullying, friendship, and justice. The webtoon follows the lives and struggles of Gray Yeon and his friends, who are constantly targeted by the bullies and gangs at their school. Gray, who is small and weak in appearance, but strong and smart in reality, decides to fight back against the bullies and protect his friends. Along the way, he meets and clashes with various characters, such as Ben Park, the leader of the Big Four, Eugene Gale, the mastermind behind the chaos, and Donald Na, the mysterious transfer student
