    Alana Cerrone ABC 6 Career

    She joined ABC6 in July 2013. She began as a weekend reporter. She then worked her way from there to a weekday reporter and later a weekend anchor. She finally settled her spot as a weeknight evening anchor.

    Throughout her time at ABC6, Alana has covered a wide range of important stories. She uncovered a loophole in Rhode Island child neglect law. She also covered live updates from the Republican headquarters during Rhode Island’s 2018 gubernatorial election. She did a live coverage from Atlanta, Georgia, for the Patriots’ appearance in Super Bowl 53 on 4 February 2019.
