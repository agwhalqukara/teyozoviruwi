How do you propagate crepe myrtle?

22 Aug 2024, 00:00

Answer Cuttings should be dipped in rooting hormone on both ends before being placed in a container of damp sand and potting mix approximately three to four inches (10 cm) deep.. Maintain moisture by wrapping them in a plastic bag. Within 4-8 weeks, rooting will have taken place. A similar question pertains to the process of propagating a crape myrtle from a cutting. Cuttings should be 4-inches long and 1/4-inch thick at the top end, with a bud 1/4-inch from the end.
