    25 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Elon Musk has something to say to another billionaire, Jeff Bezos. These two seem to have a beef and some people are really enjoying seeing this expensive online quarrel.

    The Shade Room notes ‘many would think that the top two wealthiest men in the world wouldn’t have time to engage in mild beef and throw a little shade—but #ElonMusk clearly had some time recently to gather #JeffBezos. During a recent interview, Elon Musk (the wealthiest man in the world) said that Jeff Bezos (the world’s second wealthiest man) should “work harder” and spend “less time in the hot tub” if he wants to boost his Blue Origin space program.’

    TSR cited info from New York Post and said that ‘while accepting his honor for being named ‘Person Of The Year’ by the ‘Financial Times,’ Elon Musk decided to throw a few jabs at his fellow billionaire rival Jeff Bezos. Musk didn’t hold back when speaking about what Bezos needs to do to get his coins up in order to knock him from being the wealthiest man in the world. He advised that Bezos should devote more time to his space company Blue Origin and “less time in the hot tub” if he wants to lead in the billionaire space travel race—and he should “work harder.”’

    Elon Musk also said that Jeff Bezos ‘does not seem to be willing to spend mental energy getting into the details of engineering’ and that while Bezos possesses ‘reasonably good engineering aptitude…he takes himself a bit too seriously.’

    Someone said: ‘First his lil spat with Zuck, now he’s bickering with Jeff. Elon don’t mind a quarrel.’

    A follwoer poset this: ‘Lawd not the billionaires beefing. What is the world coming too,’ and a commenter said: ‘Billionaire beef most certainly let me mind my middle class business.’

    One other commenter said: ‘This conversation is out of my tax bracket so ima mind my business,’ and a follower said: ‘Tax bracket talk…. This ain’t nothing for me to comment on.’
